Administrative Assistant

  • Provides comprehensive administrative support to ensure efficient operation of the office, including managing correspondence, scheduling appointments, and maintaining organized filing systems. Assists with data entry, document preparation, and report generation to support team activities and decision-making. Answers phone calls and greets visitors, directing inquiries to appropriate staff and ensuring professional communication at all times. Coordinates meetings, prepares agendas, and takes minutes as required. Maintains office supplies and equipment, placing orders and handling service requests as needed. Supports various departments with day-to-day administrative tasks and helps uphold organizational procedures and standards.
