Community Life Skills Support Worker

  • Client Support and Care Planning: Participates as part of a multi-disciplinary team to support mental health clients in group living and community integration. Monitors, reviews, and updates individualized care plans regularly, adjusting as needed to meet evolving client needs. Provides one-on-one and group support focused on life management skills, independent living, wellness, recreation, and vocational goals.
  • Daily Living and Health Support: Assists clients with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) to enhance independence and functional ability. Supports clients with attending appointments, arranging or providing transportation, and ensuring medication is administered as scheduled. Monitors medication effectiveness and side effects, encourages personal hygiene, and identifies changes in clients' mental or physical health.
  • Housing and Environment Maintenance: Ensures group living homes remain safe and clean through regular maintenance and cleaning in collaboration with clients. Reports maintenance issues and liaises with management for external services like pest control, snow removal, and landscaping. Performs routine housekeeping duties to maintain a safe and comfortable living environment.
  • Community Integration and Transition Support: Encourages community involvement and skill development to foster independence and help clients transition to more independent or supportive housing. Facilitates movement into and out of group homes, supports the development of peer and family networks, and works with community partners as needed.
  • Financial Administratie Duties: Reviews monthly rent arrears reports, working with clients and income support services to resolve financial issues. Maintains accurate documentation of client interactions, daily logs, summaries, and reports as required by organizational policies. Collaborates consistently with the client care team and management to ensure coordinated care and effective service delivery.
