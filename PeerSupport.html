Peer Support

  • Client Engagement and Program Development: Reviews referrals from probation and parole to determine client eligibility and collaborates with clients to develop individualized learning plans based on identified needs and assessments. Develops and maintains program guidelines to ensure consistent and effective service delivery.
  • Data Collection and Reporting: Collects and tracks program outcome data to meet organizational and funding requirements. Accurately documents client interactions, creates daily logs, summaries, and completes required reports in line with organizational policies and procedures.
  • Peer Support Recruitment and Training: Recruits, trains, and coaches peer supporters, ensuring they are well-equipped to assist program participants. Schedules, organizes, facilitates, and evaluates peer supporter training sessions, and maintains up-to-date records and reports related to peer support activities.
  • Peer Support Delivery and Advocacy: Promotes the peer support model and provides individual or group peer support to clients in the program. Supports peer supporters in decision-making processes and contributes to focus groups for co-designing curriculum content.
  • Quality and Standards Compliance: Participates in meeting accreditation standards and performs additional tasks as assigned by management to support overall program success.
