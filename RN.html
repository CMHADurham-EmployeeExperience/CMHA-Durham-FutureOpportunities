Registered Nurse

  • Clinical Care: Provides full-scope nursing services including physical and mental health assessments, care planning, medication administration (including IMs), venipuncture, ECGs, and addiction support.
  • Health Education: Educates clients on health topics like medication, disease prevention, and lifestyle, promoting informed self-care.
  • Collaboration & Leadership: Shares evidence-based practices with the team, directs complex care plans, and connects clients to healthcare and community resources.
  • Risk & Policy Management: Minimizes risk by following protocols, monitoring client status, performing medication reconciliations, and supporting policy development.
  • Professional Standards: Adheres to organizational and CNO standards, including documentation, ethics, and professional conduct.
