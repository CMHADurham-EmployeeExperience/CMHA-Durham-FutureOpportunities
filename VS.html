Vocational Specialist

  • Case Management Services: Works as part of a high-performance, multi-disciplinary team managing a shared caseload of individuals with severe and persistent mental illness across various community settings. Develops, implements, evaluates, and revises individualized treatment goals and care plans in collaboration with clients and team members. Conducts comprehensive, client-centered assessments covering psychiatric, physical, social, educational, and family-related factors. Documents client interactions using clinical methods in accordance with organizational policies. Delivers and monitors medications, ensuring clients understand services and agreements related to ACT support. Supports clients in maintaining independent living, ensuring their basic needs—such as food, clothing, and shelter—are met. Assists clients in attending appointments, arranging transportation, and developing peer and family support networks. Establishes therapeutic relationships and advocates for clients and their families while collaborating with external service providers for continuity of care. Provides after-hours crisis intervention and supports recreational, vocational, and concurrent disorder-related learning opportunities.
  • Vocational Services: Works one-on-one with clients to assess capabilities and set employment, educational, or volunteer goals. Helps clients address personal, social, or behavioral challenges affecting vocational or academic progress. Gathers information on employment history, education, and career goals while identifying barriers to employment. Develops and revises individualized vocational plans in collaboration with clients and the care team. Provides or arranges testing and training relevant to vocational development and identifies job, education, or volunteer opportunities. Offers guidance to staff, families, and employers on vocational concerns and facilitates education and career planning workshops. Participates in professional development through meetings, conferences, and workshops, sharing updated knowledge with organizational staff.
