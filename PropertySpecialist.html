Property Specialist

  • Communication & Administrative Support: Acts as the main point of contact for property-specific queries and supports the Property Management team by monitoring and forwarding emails appropriately. Maintains and develops filing systems for all properties, performs data entry including statistical input and invoice processing, and generates reports from internal systems as needed.
  • Property Maintenance & Unit Setup: Prepares work orders for landlords, contractors, and other stakeholders, and creates quotes for necessary repairs, furniture, and equipment. Oversees forensic unit setups by ordering furniture and supplies, coordinating deliveries, and arranging setup to ensure move-in readiness. Conducts daily inspections to ensure properties remain clean, functional, and accessible.
  • Project & Inventory Coordination: Assists in coordinating logistics for building projects, renovations, and related activities. Conducts audits and manages departmental and office inventory orders, while ensuring annual updates to contact lists, landlord lists, and other documentation are completed.
  • Tenants Relation & Legal Coordination: Coordinates and completes unit inspections, posts legal notices, attends client evictions, and manages occupancy agreement signings and key distribution. Acts as liaison with the organization's Paralegal on property-related matters and takes an active role in all Landlord and Tenant Board proceedings.
  • Access, Payments, & Committee: Maintains responsibility for room master key storage and its contents, and ensures proper handling and security. Oversees unit maintenance including the collection of bi-monthly payments and participates in Housing Status Meetings and various internal/external committees or working groups as assigned.
