Addictions and Mental Specialist

  • Case Management Services: Collaborates as part of a multidisciplinary team managing individuals with severe mental illness across community settings. Develops, implements, evaluates, and revises treatment goals and client care plans with clients and team members, ensuring plans are flexible to meet changing needs. Conducts comprehensive client assessments covering psychiatric, physical, social, and functional aspects. Documents client contacts using clinical methods within organizational guidelines. Delivers medications, monitors their effects, and ensures clients understand and agree to service expectations. Provides life skills training, supports clients in maintaining independence, and assists with basic needs and appointment management. Helps clients build peer and family support networks and advocates on their behalf. Collaborates with community resources to ensure appropriate and continuous care. Provides crisis intervention support during off-hours as needed.
  • Addictions and Mental Health Services: Participates in comprehensive addiction and mental health assessments within the community. Coordinates with external services for client detoxification and links clients to self-help, outpatient, and residential programs. Offers individual and group addiction treatment both in office and community settings. Provides addiction-related training and support to organizational staff. Acts as liaison between mental health and substance abuse services to facilitate client care. Attends professional development activities to maintain and share current knowledge. Develops and updates treatment goals and plans collaboratively with clients and team members.
