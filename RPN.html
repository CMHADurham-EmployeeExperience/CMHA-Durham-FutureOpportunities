Registered Nurse

  • Clinical Care & Assessment: Provides nursing services to clients with severe mental illness, including comprehensive assessments (e.g., MSEs), monitoring vitals, administering medication, and developing individualized care plans.
  • Support to Practitioners & Staff: Assists Nurse Practitioners, educates clients on treatment/medication, and supports CMHA staff through health-related training, consultations, and in-service education.
  • Health Education & Promotion: Delivers health teaching on chronic illness, medication compliance, healthy living, and disease prevention.
  • Clinical Procedures: Performs nursing interventions including venipuncture, IM medication delivery, and supports specialized services such as the clozapine clinic.
  • Care Coordination: Liaises with physicians and community providers, facilitates access to health services, and participates in discharge planning through committees like the Community Access Committee.
  • Quality & Policy Participation: Enagegs in organizational development through Nursing Council, contributes to policy creation, accreditation processes, and ensures adherence to CMHA protocols and standards.
