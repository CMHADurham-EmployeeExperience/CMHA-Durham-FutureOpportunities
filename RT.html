Recreatioanl Therapist

  • Case Management Services: The role involves working within a multi-disciplinary team to manage a caseload of clients in various community settings. Responsibilities include conducting client assessments and developing individualized care and treatment plans in collaboration with clients and team members. The position supports clients in building life skills, achieving personal goals, and maintaining independent living. Advocacy and connection to appropriate community resources are key, along with accurate documentation and providing on-call or backup support as needed.
  • Recreational Therapy Services: This role includes assessing client needs and delivering therapeutic activities, life skills training, and support for daily living. The therapist collaborates with medical, mental health, and community services to ensure well-rounded care. They also provide internal staff training and mentorship to Recreational Therapy students, while maintaining ethical standards and professional conduct.
  • Risk Management: The role requires proactive identification and management of risks in alignment with organizational policies to ensure the safety and well-being of clients, staff, and other stakeholders.
